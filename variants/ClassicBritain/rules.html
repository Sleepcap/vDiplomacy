The normal rules of Diplomacy apply except as amended below:

  1. Clyde, Wales, and Yorkshire are home supply centers for England (in addition to London, Liverpool, and Edinburgh).
  2. England begins the game with an army located in each of its six home supply centers. For any English unit to leave the home island, it must be convoyed by a foreign fleet.
  3. Due to the additional three supply centers, the victory condition is nineteen (19) centers.
